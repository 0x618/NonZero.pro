Michael Beck

Penetration Tester | Instructor

Michael Beck is an experienced Senior Penetration Tester with a passion for offensive security, reverse engineering, and development. He has spent the past three years working as a penetration tester, providing high-touch consulting services to companies within a wide range of industries: finance, healthcare, government, defense, and everything in between. Prior to his pentesting career, he worked primarily in cybersecurity research, both in the defense industry and at North Dakota State University.

Aside from penetration testing, Michael leads live OSCP trainings as an OffSec authorized instructor. His next class is Penetration Testing with Kali Linux, which will be held in-person at BlackHat USA 2024 in partnership with OffSec and Applied Technology Academy.
